PRIVACY POLICY FOR FLOWTASK EXTENSION
=====================================
Last Updated: December 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
---------------
FlowTask ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. 
This privacy policy explains how FlowTask handles your data.

2. DATA COLLECTION
------------------
FlowTask does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data created within FlowTask is stored LOCALLY on your device using 
your browser's localStorage. This includes:
- Tasks and to-do items
- Timeline entries
- Sticky notes
- Custom quotes
- Theme preferences
- Color customizations
- Productivity streaks

3. DATA STORAGE
---------------
- All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage
- Data never leaves your device
- Data is not sent to any external servers
- Data is not shared with any third parties
- We have no access to your data

4. DATA DELETION
----------------
You can delete all FlowTask data at any time by:
- Clearing your browser's localStorage
- Uninstalling the extension
- Using the browser's "Clear browsing data" feature

5. PERMISSIONS USED
-------------------
FlowTask requests the following permissions:

- "storage": To save your tasks, notes, and preferences locally on your device
- "chrome_url_overrides" (newtab): To replace your new tab page with FlowTask dashboard

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
-----------------------
FlowTask does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

The only external connection is to Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to load 
the Epilogue font family for the user interface.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
---------------------
FlowTask does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
-------------------------
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be 
reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

9. CONTACT
----------
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue 
on our GitHub repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store.

10. CONSENT
-----------
By using FlowTask, you consent to this privacy policy.

=====================================
Summary: FlowTask stores all data locally on your device. 
We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data.
=====================================
